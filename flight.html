Flight School

Learn more about Arkansas Aerials Flight School, coming soon!

Arkansas Aerials is currently in the process of locating a suitable plot of acreage near by West Little Rock to begin instructing enthusiasts in the basics of radio control flight, safety, using RC vehicles for HD video and photography, and flying techniques to set your footage apart from the rest of the amateur GoPro multi-rotor brigade.

We will be offering several different packages from beginner to advanced pilots and depending on your skill level, and we may even invite you to become part of the team. Our RCAP operation is Arkansas based but we travel all around the USA.

Watch for a press release, more information coming soon!
